Audience Description

The audience will mostly be locals wanting to get a weather report and current events. Another user of the site will be visitors to a city that will want to know what the weather will be like when they visit.

Personas

Fred Jackson

Occupation: Accountant


Fred loves the outdoors and has started to venture out to new places for climbing, hiking and various other activites. He worries a lot about the weather and is constantly looking at what the weather is like where he will take his next adventure.

Stanley Mortimer

Occupation: Retired


Stanley has always been someone that worries a lot about family. His oldest granddaughter move to a part of the country that has very cold and harsh winters. This is new to his granddaughter as she grew up in the same town as him and that makes him worry about her first winter in her new town. He is constantly wanting to know what the weather is and when a possible storm might come so he can warn her.

Scenarios

1) Local wanting to know what the weather will be like on the weekend so they can make weekend plans.

2) Someone who is hearing around the water cooler that they expect bad weather and they want to find out from a trusted source what the weather will be.

3) A relative that wants to know what the weather is like where a loved one lives. This will be very useful during the winter.
